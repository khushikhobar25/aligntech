Project overview

‍

Optima Services, a fast-growing consulting firm, faced challenges in managing client relationships and tracking sales opportunities efficiently. They needed a customized CRM system to streamline communication, track client interactions, and improve sales management.

‍

We collaborated closely with Optima’s sales and customer service teams to create a CRM solution that was tailored to their specific business processes and growth ambitions.

‍

How we Solve the problem

‍

  • Conducted workshops with key stakeholders to understand CRM requirements.
  • Designed a user-friendly CRM interface to ensure easy adoption.
  • Integrated email and calendar syncing for streamlined communication.
  • Implemented automated workflows to reduce manual data entry.
  • Built detailed reporting tools to analyze sales performance and client engagement.

‍

‍

The Result of the project and how benefited our client

‍

The custom CRM system transformed Optima Services’ operations, enabling them to manage client data more effectively and close deals faster.

‍

  • Reduced manual data entry by 40%, saving time for the sales team.
  • Increased sales by 25% due to better tracking of leads and opportunities.
  • Improved client retention with enhanced communication and follow-ups.
  • Streamlined internal processes, boosting overall productivity.
  • Provided management with actionable insights for data-driven decisions.
